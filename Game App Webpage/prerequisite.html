Chess Game

Chess is a two-player strategic board game played on an 8×8 grid called a chessboard, consisting of 64 alternating light and dark squares. It is one of the world’s oldest and most popular strategy games, combining tactics, planning, and psychological insight.

How to Play Chess

King

The king moves one square in any direction. Protect your king at all times.

Queen

The queen can move any number of squares in any direction — straight or diagonal.

Rook

The rook moves any number of squares horizontally or vertically.

Bishop

The bishop moves diagonally across the board.

Knight

The knight moves in an L-shape: two squares in one direction and then one sideways.

Pawn

The pawn moves forward one square but captures diagonally. On its first move, it can move two squares.
